Healthy Lifestyle

🥗 What is Healthy Food?

Healthy food means foods that provide essential nutrients your body needs:

  • Vitamins & minerals for immunity and body function
  • Protein for muscles and repair
  • Carbohydrates for energy
  • Healthy fats for brain and heart health
  • Fiber for digestion

These usually come from natural, less processed foods.

🍎 Main Food Types

1. Fruits & Vegetables

  • Rich in vitamins, minerals, and fiber
  • Help protect against diseases
  • Try to eat different colors daily

2. Protein Foods

  • Build muscles and repair the body
  • Chicken, fish, eggs, beans, lentils, and nuts
  • Mix animal and plant proteins

3. Carbohydrates

  • Main energy source
  • Choose whole grains
  • Avoid too much sugar and refined carbs

🥑 More Healthy Choices

4. Healthy Fats

  • Important for brain and heart health
  • Found in olive oil, avocado, nuts, and fish
  • Limit fried and processed fats

5. Dairy or Alternatives

  • Provides calcium for bones
  • Milk, yogurt, cheese, or plant-based alternatives

⚖️ What is a Balanced Diet?

A balanced diet means eating the right proportion of all food groups:

  • 🥦 Half your plate: vegetables and fruits
  • 🍗 One quarter: protein
  • 🍞 One quarter: whole grains
  • 🥑 Add small amounts of healthy fats

💡 Simple Daily Healthy Habits

  • Drink plenty of water 💧
  • Eat more home-cooked meals
  • Reduce sugar, fast food, and soda
  • Do not skip meals
  • Control portion sizes

🎯 Why It Matters

  • Stay energetic and focused
  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Prevent diseases like diabetes and heart disease
  • Improve mood and mental health
